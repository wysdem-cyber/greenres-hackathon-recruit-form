# Recruitment: Join the Climate Habit Initiative

Welcome to the team! We are building a solution for the upcoming climate hackathon, focusing on building sustainable habits for a greener future.

## About the Project
Our team is developing a platform designed to track, encourage, and maintain climate-positive behaviors. We have shifted our focus from large-scale infrastructure to user-centric habit formation, and we are looking for dedicated contributors to help us reach our goals.

## Roles We Are Seeking
We are looking for team members with interest or experience in:
* **Research:** Identifying climate impact data and behavioral psychology insights.
* **UI/UX Design:** Creating intuitive interfaces that make sustainability accessible.
* **Development:** Building the core functionality (Frontend/Backend/Mobile).
* **Strategy & Marketing:** Helping us position our project effectively for the hackathon judges.

## Why Join Us?
* Gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced hackathon environment.
* Work on a project with tangible environmental impact.
* Collaborate with a motivated team of peers.
